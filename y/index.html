Security and Privacy 💰

We made it our mission to provide the best security combined with ease of use. With Zero message store policy and encrypted data sync.

Works on secured Realtime database 📫

  • Roots uses Google database power to boost.
  • Powerful, Secured, Fast.
  • Roots Realtime data sync for fast response.
  • You can send media and files, without any limits on their type and size.

No Messages Store Policy. 📦

  • We believe privacy is a most important factor.
  • Roots Message deletes from server as soon as user receives it.
  • *Messages means the chats in chat room.

Option to Block a user and Report for inappropriate conduct. 🔐

  • Have complete control on your actions.
  • *Roots Block results in unfriend of that user and hides your profile info from getting accessed by that user.
  • *If more than 20 Reports for inappropriate conduct, may results in user account removal.
  • Feel free to contact us on any matter.
